Apply for Admission

Begin your child's journey at Floraville School today. Fill out our admission form and secure a place for your child.

How to Apply

  1. Click the Apply Now button to open the admission form.
  2. Fill in the student's name, parent details, email address, and preferred class.
  3. Submit the form so our admissions team can review your request.
  4. Prepare the required documents, including a birth certificate, passport photograph, and previous school records.
  5. Wait for a confirmation message from the school with the next steps for screening and enrollment.
